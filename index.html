Answered Questions

Everything you need to know about Rewarder

How do I join a competition?

Locate the webapp , create an account, browse available events in your talent category, pay the entry fee, and submit your performance according to the event guidelines.

How much is the entry fee?

Entry fees vary by event type and prize pool. Most events range from 10 to 150 Talent coin to enter. Premium events with larger prize pools may have higher entry fees.

How does voting work?

Users can vote for their favorite performers by paying a small fee (usually 10 to 150 Talent coinper vote). The more votes you get, the higher your chances of winning. You can share your performance link with friends and family to gather more votes.

How are winners selected?

Winners are determined by the total number of votes received during the competition period. The top 3 performers with the most votes win 1st (60%), 2nd (25%), and 3rd (15%) places respectively.

How do I receive my prize money?

Prize money is transferred to your Rewarder wallet within 7 days after the competition ends. You can then withdraw to your bank account, or other supported payment methods.

What talent categories are available?

We currently support singing, dancing, comedy, tech pitches, visual arts, cooking, fashion design, spoken word, instrumental music, and more. New categories are added regularly based on user demand.
